Insights

AI-Powered Content Platform for Ingestion, Analysis & Social Media Publishing

Turn RSS feeds, podcasts, YouTube videos, and articles into summaries, generated articles, and platform-ready social posts. One pipeline from ingestion to publishing—with transcription, summarization, and scheduling built in.

View on GitHub README

Features

RSS & YouTube – Subscribe to feeds or paste video URLs; transcribe and analyze.
Summaries & actions – AI summaries and extracted action items from any content.
Article generation – Turn content into blog posts, news articles, or deep-dives.
Social copy – Platform-optimized posts for LinkedIn, Threads, Twitter, Facebook, and more.
Scheduling – Time slots, daily limits, and background publishing.
JIRA – Create tickets from action items with full context.

Use cases

  • Content creators repurposing podcasts and YouTube videos into articles and social posts
  • Marketing teams keeping a consistent social presence from any source
  • Researchers extracting summaries and action items from interviews or lectures
  • Social managers generating and queuing posts from URLs or saved sources
  • Converting meetings or long-form video into JIRA tickets and shareable content
