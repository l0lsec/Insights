Requirements: Instagram publishing needs a professional account (Business or Creator — switch in the Instagram app under Settings → Account type and tools). Feed posts also require an image (JPEG, publicly hosted, aspect ratio between 4:5 and 1.91:1) — text-only posts are not supported by the Instagram API.
Step 1: Create a Meta Developer App

To connect Instagram you need a Meta Developer App with the Instagram product enabled (the "Instagram API with Instagram Login" setup — no Facebook Page required).

  1. Go to the Meta Developer Portal and log in with your Facebook account.
  2. Click "Create App".
  3. Choose a Use Case:
    • Select "Other", then click Next.
    • Select "Business" as the app type, then click Next.
  4. Give your app a name (e.g. "Insights Instagram") and click "Create App".
  5. On the app dashboard, scroll to "Add Products", find "Instagram" and click "Set Up".
  6. Under the Instagram product, choose "API setup with Instagram login".
Step 2: Configure Your App Settings
  1. In the left sidebar, go to Instagram > API setup with Instagram login:
    • Copy the Instagram App ID — this is your INSTAGRAM_APP_ID.
    • Copy the Instagram App Secret — this is your INSTAGRAM_APP_SECRET.
    • Note: these differ from the Meta app's own ID/secret — use the ones shown under the Instagram product.
  2. In "Set up business login", add your OAuth redirect URL (must be HTTPS):
    {{ request.url_root }}instagram/callback
Step 3: Add Yourself as a Tester

While the app is in Development mode, only invited testers can authorize. For a single-user tool this is all you need — no App Review required:

  1. In the Meta Developer dashboard, go to Instagram > Roles (or App Roles > Roles).
  2. Click "Add People", choose Instagram Tester, and enter your Instagram username.
  3. Accept the tester invitation from the Instagram app or website:
    • Open Instagram → Settings → Website Permissions → Apps and Websites → Tester Invites.
    • Accept the pending invite.
Step 4: Set Environment Variables & Connect
  1. Add the following to your .env file: 
    INSTAGRAM_APP_ID=your_instagram_app_id_here
INSTAGRAM_APP_SECRET=your_instagram_app_secret_here
INSTAGRAM_REDIRECT_URI={{ request.url_root }}instagram/callback
  2. Restart Insights so the new variables are loaded.
  3. Return to the Schedule page and click "Connect Instagram".
  4. Authorize the app when prompted — you will be redirected back automatically.
